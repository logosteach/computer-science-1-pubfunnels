Comparator Operators

In this lesson, we will explore the six comparator operators in Python and how they are used to compare values and make decisions in code. We will also reflect on how the concept of comparison is relevant when discerning between what is good, true, and aligned with God's will for us in Christ Jesus. (1 Thessalonians 5:21; Philippians 4:8).

Objectives

By the end of the lesson, learners will be able to:

  • use the six comparator operators in Python: ==, !=, >, <, >=, <=.

  • write conditions that compare numbers, strings, and variables.

  • predict the Boolean result of comparator expressions.

  • understand how comparators are used to make decisions in code.

  • recognize that just as we compare values in code, Scripture calls us to test and discern what is good, true, and aligned with God’s will (1 Thessalonians 5:21; Philippians 4:8).
