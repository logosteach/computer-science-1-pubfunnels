# Welcome to Computer Science

Hello! My name is **Mr. John Partridge**. I am first and foremost **in Christ Jesus by faith alone**. Additionally, I am a husband to my beautiful wife Karen, and father to five amazing blessings given in the Lord. My family and I live in Maine.

Currently, I am the founder, maintainer, and sole employee of [LogosTeach](https://www.logosteach.com) — a ministry organization dedicated to providing Christian educational materials anchored in the Lord Jesus Christ.

> “I have been a teacher of mathematics and computer science for over twenty years. Sharing what I have learned has always been my passion.
>
> My philosophy of learning is that students should focus on using what they learn to honor and glorify Christ, while also understanding what Christ has done for them by bringing them true rest. We now have peace with God through Him. All true, worthwhile work stems from a position of rest — the rest we have in Christ is real and effective.
>
> While this course is not a comprehensive Bible lesson, it meaningfully integrates biblical concepts and teachings from Scripture. This helps students build a solid foundation for a lifetime of learning grounded in a Christ-centered worldview.
>
> In this course, you will learn the Python programming language at a beginner level. You will also be challenged to solve many problems independently using Python code, which will strengthen your problem-solving skills.
>
> I am grateful that you have chosen to join me in this multi-week beginner course, and I am excited to be your partner and guide on this learning journey.
>
> Let’s get started!”
